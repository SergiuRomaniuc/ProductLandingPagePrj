About Us

The Sweet Story Behind Cornetti

The Birth of an Idea

Every great product starts with a simple dream. Cornetti was born from a love affair with the perfect ice cream experience. We noticed something missing in Moldova's dessert scene - a waffle cone that could truly complement the joy of ice cream, not just hold it.

Why Cornetti?

The idea came to us during a summer evening when we were enjoying ice cream with friends. The cone broke, the ice cream melted, and the magic moment was lost. That's when we realized: what if there was a cone so perfectly crafted, so deliciously crunchy, that it would enhance every single bite instead of just being an afterthought?

Our Mission

We set out to create more than just a waffle cone. We wanted to craft an experience. Every Cornetti cone is designed with extra crunch that holds up to any scoop, lined with rich chocolate that adds sweetness to every bite, and shaped perfectly to make ice cream lovers smile.

What Makes Us Different

  • Extra Crunchy Formula: Our unique recipe ensures your cone stays crispy from first bite to last
  • Chocolate-Lined Perfection: Every cone is lovingly lined with premium chocolate
  • Made for Moldova: We're bringing this beloved treat to Moldovan ice cream lovers for the first time
  • Quality First: Using only premium ingredients because you deserve the best

Our PromiseOur Promise

At Cornetti, we believe that every moment with ice cream should be pure joy. That's why we've dedicated ourselves to creating the cone that makes every bite better. When you choose Cornetti, you're not just getting a waffle cone - you're getting a promise of "Joy every bite."
